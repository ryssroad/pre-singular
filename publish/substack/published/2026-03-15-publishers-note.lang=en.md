# Publisher’s Note

### Logging the pre-singular moment

(Share image: https://substack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com/public/images/936b3a59-0897-41cb-948e-9962d178c6a7_1536x1024.png)

First rule of good ops: when a system gets unstable, you turn the logging up — not down.

The more unstable the system, the more you need a trace you can audit later, because the bug that breaks everything rarely announces itself.

That’s the posture of this publication.

We’re not here for vibes.

We’re here for continuity: scoped claims, visible edits, and receipts you can inspect over time.

Call it what it is:

pre-singular.log

`00:00Z  INFO  the future is already running`
