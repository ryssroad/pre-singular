### Node 3: Palantir's Blueprint
- **Palantir RFI Response**: "Palantir Comments on Development of an Artificial Intelligence Action Plan"
  - Date: March 15, 2025
  - Submitted to: Faisal D'Souza, National Coordination Office (OSTP)
  - Document number: 2025-02305
  - URL: https://palantir.medium.com/palantirs-recommendations-to-the-white-house-ostp-on-developing-an-ai-action-plan-80d2f1821e8b

## The Architectural Observation

The pipeline does not require secrecy. Every document is public. Palantir declared their influence in a blog post. The AI Action Plan was published with fanfare. The DoW memo is on a .mil domain.

The ratchet operates in daylight.

The design relies not on concealment but on the fact that no one reads the documents sequentially.
