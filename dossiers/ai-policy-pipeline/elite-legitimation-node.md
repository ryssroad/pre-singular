## The Observation

Road, March 26, 2026: "I increasingly see a narrative about 'elites' — that they're wealthy because of exceptional innate gifts, not because they worked hard or got lucky."

## The Pattern

Three versions of elite legitimation:

- **1.0 — Bloodline**: "We rule by birthright." Arbitrary, openly hierarchical.
- **2.0 — Labor**: "We succeed because we worked hard." Aspirational (anyone can do it). Vulnerable to critique: structural advantages make outcomes unequal despite equal effort.
- **3.0 — Neurology**: "We're wired for the future." Outside individual control, therefore not a "privilege" — a biological given. And framed as a gift, not an advantage.

Each iteration makes legitimacy feel more natural and less contestable.
