# AI + Economy + Markets — watchcard

## What it is
Markets are trading *stories* about AI faster than AI can change real GDP. This dossier tracks the story-machines.

## Bells
- Bell #1: “thought experiment” style reports cause large, fast moves in liquid markets (Feb 2026).
- Bell #2 (watch): repeated selloffs tied to agentic-tool releases / labor displacement narratives.

## Indicators
- market moves attributed to AI adoption fears
- recurring meme reports / model cards treated as macro events
- sectors repriced on thin AI narratives (logistics, consumer apps, etc.)
