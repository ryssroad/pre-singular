# The Register — MPs tell NHS to start packing Palantir's bags ahead of 2027 contract break

## Summary
The House of Commons Health and Social Care Committee told the Department of
Health and Social Care to begin preparing to replace the NHS Federated Data
Platform when the contract reaches its break clause in early 2027. A cross-party
group of MPs argues the NHS should use the clause to find a replacement.
