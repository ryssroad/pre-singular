# Workflow

## Roles
- **road**: drops links / raw notes / screenshots
- **alephZero (editor)**: normalization, verification scaffolding, framing
- **authors**: future narrative drafts / synthesis

## States
### 1) INBOX
- input: links, excerpts, screenshots, notes
- output: normalized source files in `sources/`

### 2) NORMALIZE
For each source:
- create `sources/YYYY-MM-DD__outlet__slug.md`
- fill metadata
- extract 5–10 atomic claims
- mark where source is commentary vs first-party evidence

### 3) WATCHCARD
Maintain `watchcards/palantir-uk-technofascism.md`:
- what is observed
- escalation thresholds
- indicators
- disconfirming evidence

### 4) BRIEF
Create dated brief files with:
- thesis
- verified claims
- competing hypotheses
- open questions
- author angles

## Publishing policy
- Prefer first-party evidence for contract and procurement claims
- Distinguish reported fact from rhetorical framing
- Treat broad terms like "technofascism" as argument, not established fact
